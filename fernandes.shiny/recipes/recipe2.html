Butter Chicken

  • Serving
  • Nutrition
Prep time 10 min
Cook time 20-25 min
Total time 45 min
Servings 4

This is my own version of the oreo microwave mug cake. This chocolaty treat is truly great for nights when I need a yummy dessert that is ready in less than 7 minutes! The reason it is perfect is you can have it for breakfast and also as a night snack. The times when I have a sweet tooth but don't want to overload myself with an entire chocolate cake; not that I can't finish it but I am afraid that I would have to live with the shame. For such times, I need something small, single serving and guilt free - the solution is MUG Cake.

Ingredients

  1. Chicken Breast - 200gms
  2. Onion - 2
  3. Tomatoes - 3
  4. Salt,Pepper - 2/3 tsp
  5. Curd - 25 gms
  6. Butter - 50 gms
  7. Ginger Garlic Paste - 2+2 tsp
  8. Oil - Tabelspoons
  9. Spices - 1 tbsp (red chilli powder, turmeric)
  10. Coriander - to garnish
back to top

Method

Step 1: Marinate
Cut the chicken into pieces as desired. Add Salt 1 tsp salt and 1 tsp pepper. Add 2 tbsp ginger garlic paste. Add Turmeric and Red Chilli powder 1 tsp each. Add curd. Mix it all well and set this aside for 10~15min until you prep the rest of the steps
Step 2: Chop
Start chopping onions and tomatoes fine.
Step 3: Fry
Shallow fry the above marinated chicken until the chicken turns golden brown. (You can leave the chicken half cooked here). Keep this chicken aside.
Step 4: Cook
In the same pan, add the chopped onions, and let them fry until they turn golden. Now add the chopped tomatoes and keep stiring. Add the ginger garlic paste making some space in between the pan. Start adding the spices to this mix (red chilli powder - 1 tbsp, turmeric - 1/2 tbsp) and keep mixing it well.
Step 5: Grind
Take the above cooked mix and add it to the mixer to grind until fine paste is formed.
Step 6: Boil
In the same pan, add this fine paste. Add butter to this and let it cook for 2~3min. Add the above fried chicken to this and let it come to boil. Let it steam by placing a lid to the pan if necessary for another 7~8 min on medium flame.
back to top

Serving

In a bowl serve hot and add coriander for garnishing. Can be served with a side of bread or steamed rice.
