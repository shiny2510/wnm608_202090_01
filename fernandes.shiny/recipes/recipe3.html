Banana Walnut Brownie

  • Serving
  • Nutrition
Prep time 15 min
Cook time 35 min
Total time 50 min
Servings 12

This is my own version of the oreo microwave mug cake. This chocolaty treat is truly great for nights when I need a yummy dessert that is ready in less than 7 minutes! The reason it is perfect is you can have it for breakfast and also as a night snack. The times when I have a sweet tooth but don't want to overload myself with an entire chocolate cake; not that I can't finish it but I am afraid that I would have to live with the shame. For such times, I need something small, single serving and guilt free - the solution is MUG Cake.

Ingredients

  1. banana ripe - 2
  2. Salt - a pinch
  3. Sugar - 1 cup
  4. All purpose flour - 1 cup
  5. butter melted- 1 cup
  6. cocoa powder - 1/2 cup
  7. baking powder - 1 tsp
  8. eggs - 2
  9. walnut chopped - 1/2 cup for garnishing
back to top

Method

Step 1: Smash
Smash the ripe bananas to make a fine paste
Step 2: Sugar
Add powdered sugar to this paste and mix well
Step 3: Add eggs
Break two large eggs and whisk
Step 4: Butter
Melt the butter and add it to the mix.
Step 5: Sieve
Sieve the all purpose flour, cocoa powder and baking powder to this mix and make a consistent batter.
Step 6: Layer
Add this batter to a baking tray and add chopped walnuts on the top
Step 7: Bake
Preheat Oven at 350F and then keep the baking tray in once preheated. Set the timer for 35min at 350F.

Serving

In a bowl serve hot and add your fav scoop of ice-cream(preferably vanilla)
